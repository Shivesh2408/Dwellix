## Executive Summary

Dwellix is an AI-powered Home Management Platform that combines AI diagnosis, home asset management, service booking, live tracking, payments, warranties, invoices and maintenance into one intelligent platform.

---

## Vision

To become the world's most trusted digital operating platform for home management.

---

## Mission

Help every homeowner maintain, organize and protect their home using intelligent technology.

---

## Problem Statement

Current home maintenance is fragmented.

Users struggle with:
- Finding trusted technicians
- Comparing repair prices
- Tracking repairs
- Managing warranties
- Storing invoices
- Scheduling maintenance
